The Statue of Unity is an under planning 182 metres (597 ft) monument of Vallabhbhai Patel that will be created directly facing the Narmada Dam, 3.2 km away on the river island called Sadhu Bet of Narmada river, near Bharuch in Gujarat state of India.

The unique Statue of Unity project was visioned by Gujarat CM & BJP's Prime Ministerial candidate Narendra Modi in the memory of Iron Man Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel who united India after independence in 1947.[3] At the time of independence, India was divided into 562 independent “rajwada”. Uniting 562 rajwada into one country, this mammoth task was assigned to Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel who made it possible by his unique wit and vision. Sardar Patel is referred as the architect of United India which stands today. Narendra Modi decided to pay a tribute to the Architect of India by immortalizing his memory through an enormous project of this level.
